About me

In 2021, Vigtory was acquired by fuboTV, which led me to being the Director of Engineering for fubo Gaming.

In early 2020, I joined Vigtory as engineer #1, with the goal of launching America's first reduced juice sportsbook.

From 2019 to early 2020, I was at Clarity Partners, where I led the development of a new incident reporting system for the Chicago Police Department.

In 2018, I interned at Platform9, where I contributed to the Kubernetes Cluster API by adding support for Microsoft's cloud platform, Azure.

In 2017, I interned at Clarity Partners, where I used using C# and .Net to develop software to developer software for the Chicago Police Department.

In 2016, I interned at McKesson, where I developed software in Ruby used to validate gigabytes of incoming hospital data daily.
