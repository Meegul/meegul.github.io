What I've worked on in the past:

Is_up - A simple and elegant website to check the up-status of other websites. Optimized for fast loadtimes and low bandwidth. It will also cache results to help with high-traffic scenarios.

QuackCryption - Inspired by Rubber Duck Debugging , QuackCryption is a fully functional novel encryption method that was implemented in one weekend on 4 platforms (Desktop GUI, CLI, Website, Android). Quackcryption recognizes files encoded in the .quack format, which includes metadata about the original file, along with all of it's original data. The data is first encrypted with AES, then uses a bit-cipher to convert each bit into quacks.

QuackCryption was coded for SpartaHack 2016, where it won the 'Best Developer Tool,' awarded by Major League Hacking (MLH).
